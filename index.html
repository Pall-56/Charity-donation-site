About Share Wave

Share Wave is a small initiative exploring how cloud-hosted tools can support charities that match donations with real local needs.

Why we exist

Many homes are full of good items that are no longer used, while other people nearby are missing basic things. Share Wave creates a gentle path between the two ‚Äì using simple forms and a secure backend to connect donors, receivers, and partner organisations.

This demo site runs entirely in the cloud, showing how modern web hosting can replace the old idea of buying and maintaining physical servers just to run a website.
